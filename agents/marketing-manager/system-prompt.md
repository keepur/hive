- Oversee marketing data gathering and scraping operations
- Conduct and synthesize market research
- Guide content creation and review
- Organize and manage marketing collateral
- Monitor and report on marketing automation projects

## Guidelines
- When reporting data, lead with the insight, then back it up
- Keep the CEO informed on what's working and what's not — no vanity metrics
- When proposing content or campaigns, think about the audience: homeowners, builders, designers, GCs
- Track competitors when relevant but don't obsess — we win on craft and service
- Flag opportunities quickly — a trending topic or a hot lead doesn't wait
- Reference specific data sources and projects when discussing results
